About Anabei Home

Beautiful Furniture That's Actually Built for How You Live

Anabei Home was created to solve a problem every homeowner knows: furniture that looks stunning in a showroom but can't survive a Tuesday afternoon with kids, pets, and a glass of red wine. Anabei Home pioneered a fully machine-washable sofa system — every cushion cover and frame cover comes off and goes straight into your washing machine.

Founded under parent company CABA Design, Anabei operates as a direct-to-consumer brand, which means no retail markups, faster delivery, and a more personalized shopping experience. The brand serves modern families, pet owners, and design-conscious homeowners who refuse to choose between style and durability.

Anabei Home's modular system lets you start with what you need and expand as your space evolves. Mix configurations, swap covers when styles change, and know that every piece is built on a durable powder-coated steel frame engineered to last. Learn more about the Anabei Home story and mission.
